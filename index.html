What is chatbot?

A chatbot is a computer program that simulates and processes human conversation in which uses artificial intelligence (AI) and natural language processing (NLP) to understand customer questions and automate responses to them also  It allows  humans to interact with digital devices as if they are communicating with a real person during the conversation .

Advantages:

1-Faster Customer Service.

2-Higher Customer Satisfaction.

3-Reduction in Costs of Human Resources.

4- Offering 24/7 support.

Disadvantages

1-Unable to understand human emotions during the conversation.

2-Certain chatbots are poor in processing and takes time to filter results.

3-Chatbots are poor in making decisions unlike human beings.

4-They have limited replies and solutions.

Main types of Chatbot

1

Simple chatbots

They have limited capabilities, ex: ordering pizza.

2

Smart chatbots

They are designed to simulate near-human interactions with customers, ex: virtual assistant.

3

Hybrid chatbots

They are a combination of simple and smart chatbots, Ex: Medical Diagnosis: It helps with diagnosing through asking symptoms questions.
